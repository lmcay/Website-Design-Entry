Ghibli Studio

Ghibli is a word that was chosen by miyazaki, The Word ‘Ghibli’ is an italian word that is based on a libyan term which is hot desert wind. The message of Ghibli made way creating a path on the studio blowing the new wind to the world of animation. Since then, Ghibli has been true to their title in which they shake the world by providing a good quality film, having them known in the film industry.

Ghibli History

Ghibli was founded in 1985 by directors Hayao Miyazaki, Isao Takahata and Producer Toshio suzuki. Before the studio existed Miyazaki and Takahata already had a predatory relationship

The pair worked together in 1964 creating a beginning to their collaborative relationship and they continue to work together throughout their life career.

Chihiro

Chihiro is a young girl who, while moving to a new home, stumbles into a mysterious and enchanting spirit world, where she must work in a bathhouse to save her parents from a curse.

Chihiro was inspired by the daughter of one of the director's friends. After announcing his retirement in 1997, the filmmaker took friends to his mountain cabin. His friend's daughter and her peers inspired Miyazaki, as they were on the verge of adolescence and extremely apathetic.
